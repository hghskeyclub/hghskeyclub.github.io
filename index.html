What is Key Club?

Key Club is an international, student-led organization that provides its members with opportunities to provide service, build character and develop leadership. We partner with our local Kiwanis club in order to make a positive impact within our community. From park and beach clean ups to distribution events, members learn how to take on leadership roles and see the influence they can have on the lives of others.

Our Key Club!

Here at Hialeah Gardens Sr. High we strive to provide everyone the opportunity to volunteer within our community. We hold many annual events during Halloween, Thanksgiving, the holiday season, and all throughout the school year. Our charter dates back to 2009 and for almost 2 decades positive impact on our local populace been at the forefront of our efforts.

Core Values

Leadership

Leadership is grounded in humility and service to others. A true leader listens, recognizes, and empowers.

Character Building

Character is built by everyday acts of kindness, integrity and teamwork to grow stronger relationships and communities.

Caring

Compassion for others is a cornerstone of the Key Club experience, inspiring action and service to one's community.

Inclusiveness

We welcome people of all backgrounds and ethnicities to join in serving and making a positive difference in our world.

Pledge

I pledge, on my honor, to uphold the Objects of Key Club International; to build my home, school and community; to serve my nation and world; and combat all forces which tend to undermine these institutions.

DCON

The District Education and Leadership Conference, also known as DCON is the annual District Convention for the Florida District. Here, multiple clubs around Florida submit certain projects showcasing their year or experiences. Members run for district level positions, and most importantly they have the opportunity to meet, hear from, and connect with many leaders and other members all across the Florida district.
